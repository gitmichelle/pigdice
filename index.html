Welcome to Pigu Dice!

The rules of the game are simple:

  • Each time you roll the dice, you accumulate points
  • Roll a "1" and your tally score is reset
  • Rolling a "1" means you lose your turn
  • If you pass, tallied points are added to your score
  • First to 100 wins the game!
